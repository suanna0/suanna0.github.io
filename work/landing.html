Suanna Zhong is an interdisciplinary creative working at the intersection of technology, music, and pop culture. With a background in CS, she explores how emerging tools can shape the future of media distribution and facilitate human connection.

She is pursuing a Bachelor's of Computer Science and Fine Art degree at Carnegie Mellon University. Her prior work has been commissioned by Meta/Facebook, Pinterest, Common Sense Media, and Dehancer.
