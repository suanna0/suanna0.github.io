About

Suanna Zhong is a fourth-year student in the Bachelor of Computer Science and Arts (BCSA) program at Carnegie Mellon University, studying Computer Science and Art through the School of Computer Science and the College of Fine Arts. Being in this unique position between disciplines, as well as having experience teaching CS, she hopes that her work can help technical research breakthroughs become more legible to broader audiences. Hence, she enjoys being in conversation with artists, researchers, engineers, and anyone who feels like they fall somewhere in between.

When she’s not working, you can probably find her making art, listening to jazz, cafe hopping, or knitting winter clothes.
