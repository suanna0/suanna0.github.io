Suanna Zhong is a rising senior studying Computer Science and Art at Carnegie Mellon University. Her work seeks to explore of the infinite permutations of merging art and technology–she loves being in constant conversation with artists, engineers, and people who feel that they lie somewhere in between.

Outside of work, she loves making art, cafe hopping, and knitting winter clothes.
