About

Suanna Zhong is a rising senior studying CS and Art at Carnegie Mellon University. Her work seeks to explore of the infinite permutations of combining art and technology–she loves being in constant conversation with artists, engineers, and people who feel that they lie somewhere in between.

Her clients span a wide range of industries, from tech to contemporary art and pop music, including Meta/Facebook, Pinterest, Common Sense Media, Dehancer, Marcus Rahauser, Republic Records, and DAY6.

Outside of work, she loves making art, cafe hopping, and knitting winter clothes.
