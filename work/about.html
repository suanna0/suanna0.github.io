About

Suanna Zhong is a fourth-year student studying Computer Science and Art at Carnegie Mellon University. Having studied both fine art and programming, she is interested in the unexpected ways these two disciplines can inform each other. This exploration often takes place through playing with software, building websites, color grading, or filmmaking. She enjoys being in conversation with artists, engineers, and anyone who feels like they fall somewhere in between.

When she’s not working, you can probably find her making art, listening to jazz, cafe hopping, or knitting winter clothes.
