Lunar Gala Photography

Duration: 6 months

In my sophomore year, I took on the role of Head of Photography for CMU's student run fashion club, Lunar Gala, which hosts the largest annual fashion event in Pennsylvania. The role consisted of leading a team of eight photographers, hosting ideation meetings to follow the creative team's vision, and photographing over 100+ pieces to be used as web, print, and social media assets.
