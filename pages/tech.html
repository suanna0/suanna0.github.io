My computer art can be categorized as such:

    Appreciation of labor–works such as Diagram Appropriaton aim to acknowledge the time and meticulous nature of problem-solving.
    Defining what a computer is–exploring the boundaries of computation, defining what a computer is (and isn’t), and bridging the gap between Computer Scientists and non-CS individuals. My experience teaching CS to non-coders reinforced how concepts that seem trivial to one group can be completely new to another.
    Web as a service–this includes projects like this website! :)
