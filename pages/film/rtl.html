Realtree Lesbians

Composer, Director, Editor, Colorist, 2024.

Music by Usher, The Neptunes, Suanna Zhong.

Realtree Lesbians is about two young women falling in love and running away into the forest. It is also about my mental journey confronting the heteronormative expectations other people impose on me, rejecting the nuclear family structure I was taught to follow, and choosing how I want to love the people around me.

Featured in 2024 Ovation Film Fest.
