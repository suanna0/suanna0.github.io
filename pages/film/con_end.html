A Thin Con End

Music composition, Director, Editor, Colorist, 2025.

I built a system to draw attention to my declining music abilities. A Thin Con End is a renegotiation of how music can be encoded.

Starring Viviana Staicu and Khoi Do. This film was made possible with the support of Dehancer's affiliate program.

Timeline: 3 weeks

Music Composition:

Over the years, my piano-playing abilities have only gotten worse: my left hand is weaker now, I struggle to read sheet music, and I'm too concerned with the timing of notes to even consider changes of volume throughout a song.

For this project, I was heavily inspired by Brian Eno's 1975 project, Discreet Music, where he creates a computer program that interacts with the music played by an orchestra. Eno takes on a passive role where he is unable to interfere with the software's output. However, I wanted to impose different restrictions on myself: I cannot use sheet music and must play the song from memory. Without using sheet music as an anchor of correctness, each iteration I play is bound to be very different from the previous one.
