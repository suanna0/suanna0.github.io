c0vm, An Interactive Environment

Unity, 2023

C0vm is an immersive art piece inspired by the coding assignment, c0vm, from 15-122 Principles of Imperative Computation taught at CMU. The assets used in this creation consist of 3D scans of my freshman dorm room and a bathroom in the CS Building. Accompanying these visuals are carefully selected screenshots showcasing the actual code of the c0vm assignment, as well as messages related to my experiences in 15-122.

By bringing together the realms of coding, personal experience, and auditory stimulus, c0vm encapsulates the struggles, limitations, and perseverance inherent in the world of programming, inviting viewers to explore the complexities and emotional nuances entwined within the act of creation.
