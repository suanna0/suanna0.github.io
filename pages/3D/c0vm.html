c0vm, An Interactive Environment

Unity, 2023.

C0vm is an immersive art piece inspired by the virtual machine lab from CMU course 15-122, Imperative Computation. The assets used in this piece include 3D LiDAR scans of my dorm room, a bathroom stall from the campus Computer Science building, and screenshots of code.

By bringing together the realms of coding, personal experience, and auditory stimulus, c0vm encapsulates the honest struggles, limitations, and perseverance I experienced in pursuit of a degree in Computer Science.
