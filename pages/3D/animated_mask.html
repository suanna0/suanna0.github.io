Animated Mask for Build Fantasy

ZBrush, Maya, DaVinci Resolve, 2023.

Over summer 2023, Katelyn Ma and I collaborated on a short film exploring different forms of queerness by creating alternate timelines that divert from the heteronormative nuclear family structure. In the final scene, Ceci Sun dances to 'The Swan' by Camille Saint-SaÃ«ns.

Timeline: 3 weeks

Process:

Masks serve as a motif that represent alternative expression. Much of the short film takes inspiration from plants and nature, but I also wanted to create a clean, alien-like, unfamiliar look for the final scene. My goal in modeling the mask was to create a form that found a balance between organic and sterile.

The goal of rigging was to create some muscle systems along the form as if the mask had a life of its own. However, this did not end up being necessary in the final render since the dancer moved a lot and the viewer would not be able to notice subtle changes in the mask's shape.

During filming, we mapped the nodes onto the dancer's face that corresponded with the nodes in the mask. Instead of mapping one-to-one in every frame in the video, I decided to render every other frame to emphasize the artificial quality of the mask.

The film is set to release in 2025.
