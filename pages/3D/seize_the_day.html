Seize The Day

ZBrush and Maya, 2023

Final project for CMU 60125 - Introduction to 3D Animation. Seize the Day is an exploration of the mundane emotions associated with going to high school everyday. Through this piece, I sought to evoke a sense of dullness that often comes with repetitive patterns. The process of ideation, creating assets, assembling scenes, and animating has served as an invaluable learning experience, inspiring my continued pursuit and exploration of 3D Animation.
