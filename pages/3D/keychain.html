Keychain

ZBrush, Maya, DaVinci Resolve, 2024.

Process:

I drew inspiration from collectible toys such as Smiskis, Miffy, and Astro Boy. To avoid over-complicating the form, I looked for ways to communicate a human form with as few shapes as possible. I studied the proportions of various figurines and I noticed that the combination of big head, small torso, and large feet worked well.

Final sketch before modeling.
