Irish Exit Website

Irish Exit is a group exhibition in collaboration with Bethany Hwang, Morgan Nash, and Helena Starzec in the Frame Gallery, in Pittsburgh, PA. Since the exhibition only lasted for four days, I created a simple website to immortalize it.

Timeline: 6 weeks

Typeface Pairing:

An Irish Exit is a quiet goodbye. Thus, I wanted to choose a typeface that felt peaceful, patient, but still had personality. Another challenge was to find a typeface that was bold without distracting from the art pieces.

Oddity Script has very striking design traits. To contrast the bold curves, I opted for Bookmania Light for the body.

Slow Scroll:

The slow movement and change in opacity encourages the user to slowly digest the information on the website. I experiented with different ease timings and decided on 3 seconds.
