My Faulty Machine (Under Construction)

Branding, print booklet, 2024.

Timeline: 4 weeks

My Faulty Machine is a book that illustrates introductory concepts in 15251: Theoretical Computer Science. The goal of this project is to clarify technological terms in a friendly way, furthering my investigationâ€“how can I demystify the computer?

Process:

The laws of mathematics, like Nature, is a timeless constant that we all experience against our own will. Drawing inspiration of how Nature is represented in various pieces of media, I choose to view the abstract computer as a bold, assertive, feminine figure. Hence, I used hot pink as an accent color in my design.

Embodying the concept of "demystifying" the computer, I experimented with various types of transparent papers such as tracing paper, acetate, and translucent yupo.

Since I wanted the book to lay flat, I opted for coptic binding, piercing holes through every signature.
