Cat Collector

Python, Tkinter, CMU Graphics Library, 2022.

Timeline: 3 weeks

Final project for CMU 15112–Fundamentals of Programming.

Cat Collector, inspired by the game Neko Atsume, is a simulation game with hand drawn graphics. The player buys cat toys and food to attract cats to their yard. Cats will reward the player with currency to buy more cat toys that can attract cats of different rarity.
