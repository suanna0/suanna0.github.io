Hi, I'm Suanna!

I am a multidisciplinary artist based in Pittsburgh, PA studying Computer Science and Art in the Bachelor of Computer Science and Arts program with a minor in Animation and Special Effects at Carnegie Mellon University.

I believe that similar to identity, artistic practice is never static; my work is multidimensional, always evolving, and aims to explore the intersections of contemporary art and rapidly-changing technology through a variety of materials. Recently, I've been experimenting with these mediums:

  • Code/Computationally Generated Art
  • Design/Illustration
  • 3D Animation/Digital Sculpture
  • Photography
  • Filmmaking/Directing

In my free time, I love listening to music, going thrifting, and trying new cooking recipes.
