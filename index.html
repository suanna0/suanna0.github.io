Artist Statement/CV

Suanna Zhong (b. 2004) currently resides and works in Pittsburgh, PA. With a background in drawing and photography, she explores how the inclusion and exclusion of elements shape the rectangular picture plane. Portraits of close friends, celebrities, or commercial objects are collapsed into two-dimensions and juxtaposed against traces of mathematical mark-making, connecting the tangible and invisible worlds she inhabits.
