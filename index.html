Suanna Zhong

I am interested in exploring the intersections of technology and the arts, using my technical background to amplify creative voices through digital experiences. Currently, I am studying Computer Science and Fine Art with a minor in Animation and Special Effects at Carnegie Mellon University. My work has been commissioned by Meta/Facebook, Pinterest, Common Sense Media, and Dehancer.
